PRD: Office Afternoon Slump Snack Ritual App
1. App Overview & Objectives
Overview
A calm, comforting, playful mobile experience designed to help office workers reset mentally during the afternoon slump through a frictionless snack-ordering ritual.
The app is positioned as a daily emotional reset ritual, not a logistics platform.
Objectives
•	Create a daily habit loop
•	Reduce cognitive and emotional fatigue
•	Provide fast emotional relief
•	Build ritualized usage behavior
•	Make breaks feel intentional and comforting
